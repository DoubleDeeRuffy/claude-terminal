## Note on Structure

v1.1 did not use formal `REQ-ID` traceability. Requirements were captured inline in each phase's ROADMAP entry and CONTEXT.md, not in `.gsd/REQUIREMENTS.md` (which stayed empty for the entire milestone).

The canonical v1.1 requirement list lives phase-by-phase in `.gsd/milestones/v1.1-ROADMAP.md`. For future milestones the recommended pattern is to define REQ-IDs up-front as v1.0 did.
